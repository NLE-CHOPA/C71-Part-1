Campus Life

school library

was built in the year 1980.

We believe that it takes a village to raise a reader, that’s why we love working in partnership with local schools to build resources and collections. We’re dedicated to connecting readers of all ages with books that will inspire and engage, this includes servicing Schools and Libraries. We understand that for teachers and librarians finding time to track down books can be difficult. So whether you drop into the store, with, or without an appointment, or contact us via email, we’ll endeavour to make it as straightforward for you as possible.

Won First Inter School Debate on Enviroment Pollution

St. Xaviers is another of the nation’s, and the world’s, most excellent institutions of higher education, that maintains a speech and debate team that finished in the top ten in the nation in the past year. The campus regularly hosts tournaments on campus, giving the GPDT members exposure to other teams and events related to the club—a fabulous opportunity for networking. St. Xaviers is highly invested in enriching the experiences of their speech and debate students by providing as many resources and benefits as possible in their travel and competition, and team members are regularly recognized at National Debate Tournament championships as the best in that nation.
